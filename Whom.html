Hector Lopez

Hector Lopez is currently an MA of English candidate at the University of Victoria. His research interest are on electronic literature focusing on video game narratives/narratology, digital archiving, and digital humanities with a focus on crowdsourced infromation creation.

Reuben Copley

Reuben Copley is currently in the second year of his MA in English at the University of Victoria. His research interests lie in the Medieval usage of cartography and geographical references in literature as a means of identity construction for nascent European nation states, particularly in the British Isles.
