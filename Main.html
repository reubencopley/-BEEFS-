The initial intention was to create a three column visualization. When the author link is selected an image and text would appear in the central column and the associated 'Beefs' would appear in the right column. As this project was time-limited we have instead settled for a modal box display for information. When the links are selected a modal box appears with the name of the author, a short bio, and select 'Beefs' that they may have had, or others may have had with them. Those 'Beefs' are given a -10 to 10 rating in three categories: Professional, Psychological/emotional, and Physical. Professional 'beefs' are disputes that arise from reviews or criticism, or professional rivalry. Psychological/emotional 'beefs' relate to disputes that arise from a separate psychological or emotional reaction than that of the purely professional (such as disputes or rivalries between family members or lovers). The physical 'beef' is not as obvious as it may seem at first. A physical dispute may involve direct physical contact, but in some instances may be more complex. For instance, the act of destroying a physical representation of another author's ideas is both physical and psychological and the rankings would reflect this. It is an arbitrary system that may change at a later point. As is the entire 'Beefs' project. We are amateur html/css practitioners and the current state of BEEFS reflects that. We ask you to be patient as this idea grows into something more polished. Take a look at the 'beefs' we have compiled, and enjoy the 'beefs'!
